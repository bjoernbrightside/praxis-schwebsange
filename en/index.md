# Welcome to the Website of the Praxis fir Psychotherapie Schwebsange

We offer **professional psychotherapeutic support** for all affected persons through accredited psychotherapists with several years of professional experience.

## Our Approach

We follow an **integrative psychotherapy approach**: Our therapists are trained in multiple methods and tailor treatment individually to your situation and needs. Elements of **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Schema Therapy**, etc. may be used and combined as needed.
